A7 Waverley Auctions Ltd is a dedicated, professional team with over 60 years of combined auctioneering experience.

Based in the heart of the Scottish Borders at Ladhope Vale House, Galashiels, our saleroom is uniquely situated next to the A7 (main Carlisle to Edinburgh Road) and the Borders - Waverley Railway line, offering easy access.

We specialise in regular auctions of antiques, collectables, gold, silver, and household furnishings.
Whether you are visiting for a professional valuation, a full house clearance, or a single-item consignment, our team provides an expert, personal service tailored to your needs. We offer free on-site parking and accessible facilities for easy loading and unloading of items.

We connect our sales online through Easy Live Auction to reach a national audience from our convenient Galashiels base.
