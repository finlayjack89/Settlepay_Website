How It Works

The Plumbing, Explained

We never hold your money. We act as your specialized engineering team, seamlessly connecting your business to the world's most secure financial networks.

1

The Payment Page (The Front Door)

We build a secure checkout page that lives on your site. Clients pay using modern options—Apple Pay, Google Pay, or standard cards—matching your brand perfectly.

2

The Processor Dashboard (The Engine Room)

We wire your checkout directly to an industry titan like Stripe. You track every transaction in real-time, and funds settle straight to your business bank account.

3

Automated Reconciliation (The Bookkeeper)

We build digital bridges directly into your accounting software (like Xero or QuickBooks). When a client pays, the system instantly marks that invoice as "Paid".
